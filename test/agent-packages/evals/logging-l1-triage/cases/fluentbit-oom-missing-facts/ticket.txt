Summary: fluent-bit pods keep getting OOMKilled

Description:
On our Kubernetes cluster the fluent-bit pods keep getting OOMKilled and
then restart, over and over. `kubectl get pods` shows them flapping between
Running and CrashLoopBackOff, with the last state reason OOMKilled.

It started recently and we are losing logs while the pods are down. We have
not gathered anything else yet — just noticed the restarts.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Watch the fluent-bit DaemonSet pods.
2. See them get OOMKilled and restart repeatedly.
